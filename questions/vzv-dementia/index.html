Should everybody get the VZV vaccine for dementia prevention?

Multiple epidemiological studies suggest a link between varicella zoster virus (VZV) reactivation and subsequent dementia risk as well as a reduction of dementia risk following vaccination. Most of the studies looked at older populations. The question remains if just everybody should get vaccinated against VZV to reduce their dementia risk.

Relevant Papers

  • Recombinant zoster vaccine is associated with a reduced risk of dementia.
