**Problem**: Neuroimaging manifestations of inborn errors of immunity are underrecognized, leading to delayed diagnosis of these treatable immunodeficiency disorders.

**Result**: IEIs demonstrate characteristic primary and secondary neuroimaging patterns that extend beyond infections to include diverse CNS manifestations related to immune dysregulation.

**Open Questions**: Specific imaging protocols and standardized criteria for recognizing IEI-related neuroimaging patterns in clinical practice remain to be established.
