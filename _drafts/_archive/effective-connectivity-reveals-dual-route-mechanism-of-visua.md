**Problem**: The neural circuitry implementing precision-weighted prediction in the brain's predictive processing system remained unclear despite its theoretical importance.

**Result**: High-precision predictions trigger excitatory insula-to-V1 connectivity while simultaneously activating inhibitory pathways from insula-to-pulvinar and pulvinar-to-V1, creating a dual enhancement/dampening mechanism.

**Open Questions**: How disruptions in this insula-pulvinar precision-weighting circuit contribute to specific neuropsychiatric conditions and whether these pathways represent therapeutic targets remains to be determined.
